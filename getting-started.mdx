# Getting Started with Creda

A short path to your first supply and (optional) borrow.

## 1) Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">Go to creda.finance and click Connect Wallet.</Step>
  <Step title="Pick a wallet">Keplr or Leap (browser), or WalletConnect for mobile.</Step>
  <Step title="Approve">Approve the connection and add Terra if prompted.</Step>
</Steps>

## 2) Supply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose an asset">On Dashboard, click Supply.</Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount">Review APY and impact; approve if needed, then confirm.</Step>
  <Step title="Collateral toggle">Enable only if you plan to borrow.</Step>
</Steps>

## 3) Borrow (optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select asset & amount">Keep health factor comfortably above 1.0 (aim 1.5+).</Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">Review rate and HF, then confirm.</Step>
</Steps>
