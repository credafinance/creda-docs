# What is Creda Finance?

Creda is a Cosmos‑native lending protocol. Supply assets to earn, borrow against collateral, and manage risk with clear guardrails like health factor, Isolation Mode, and EMode.

## Why Creda

- Simple: clean UI and predictable flows
- Safe by design: over‑collateralized loans, automated liquidations
- Solid pricing: multi‑source oracles with validation and smoothing

## Built For Cosmos

- CosmWasm smart contracts, low fees, IBC‑ready
- Deployed on Terra (Phoenix‑1)
